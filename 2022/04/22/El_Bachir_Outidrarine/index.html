Today I escaped bugs. Or no, I discarded them, because they were within me in my own perception - not outside.

I am Full Stack Software Engineer/UX Designer

I’ve loved helping others and solving problems for as long as I can remember, and I know it’s the general path I want to pursue.
Growing up, I had never considered pursuing computer science. Aside from my school’s unfortunate lack of CS courses, I always pictured computer science as a difficult field, and that you can have no social interaction in the CS industry. But my curiosity turned me to be a software engineer.
I started my journey as a self-thought, using online tools and various books. Since English was not my first language, the road was a bit rough. Then, after getting my High School Degree, I chose to continue my studies in Computer Science at the Agadir Higher School of Technology.

He is a Software Engineer, a self-thaught full-stack developer and UX Designer. He is also a Google Certified Project Manager. He is a Project Manager in TensorFlow User Group Agadir, and a Core Team member in GDSCUIZ.
