Bio

I am the Non Technical Lead of the Google Developer Student Clubs University of Ibn Zohr, a Project manager, and a Software Engineer currently enrolled in the third year of Mathematics and Computer Sciences at the Faculty of Sciences in Agadir.

Ever since childhood, I have always believed in the importance of entrepreneurship and technology in helping our country become one of the big players on the world stage of the modern economy. Therefore, I have always sought to challenge myself and become a crucial leader impacting our society and inspiring people in different ways.

As a result, I have always set my mindset to that of a lifelong learner. Thus, whatever the challenges I am always motivated to grow and learn from the difficulties ahead. No matter what it is, as long as I resist the urge to give up, as long as I believe that there is a way for me to improve, I take the challenge as a chance to polish my personality and skills.
